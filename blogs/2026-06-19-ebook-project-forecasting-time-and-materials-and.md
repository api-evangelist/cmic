Modern construction projects produce financial and operational data across dozens of workflows, creating both complexity and opportunity. This CMiC eBook explores how integrating forecasting, time and materials billing, and work mobilization within a single ERP database eliminates data fragmentation, improves reporting accuracy, and accelerates decision-making.
